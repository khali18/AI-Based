Health & Safety Guidelines

Learn how to check for medicine safety, authenticity, and proper disposal.

How to check authenticity

Every authentic drug dispensed through the clinic contains a unique Batch ID on its box. Always check to verify it matches our system record.

Expiry Risk Warning

Medicines close to expiry (less than 30 days) should be used under pharmacist guidance. Expired medicines should be discarded immediately and never consumed.

Safe Disposal of Expired Medicines

Do not flush expired medicines down the toilet or throw them in open dustbins. Return them to Kumasi Technical University Clinic or any registered pharmacy for safe ecological disposal.
